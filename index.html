About Me

Hello, my name is Rafael.

I like to think I'm quite a creative guy when it comes to mixing arts and technology. Currently studying Computer Science at Técnico in Lisbon, one of my goals is to enter the world of Web Development. Although I'm still a rookie, I enjoy making intuitive and asthetically pleasing stuff.

Web Dev

In 2022 I started working as a Freelancer on my first professional web dev project.

I designed and developed the website for the portuguese food brand Flovers. From the prototyping phase in Figma to the deployment on the host domain, my work included coding (html, css and javascript), graphic design, illustration and other related services.

Despite the huge responsibility of taking on a project like this one alone, it was an awesome experience that ignited my passion for design and web development.

Comics

Drawing has been a passion of mine since I was a little kid.
That passion grew into Manga, Comics and a dream of becoming a published author. So at 16 years old I had the amazing oportunity of participating in a collection of comics released by the publisher Escorpião Azul, titled "Stories from Another World".
My work here is a science fiction piece called "Second Chance".

▲ Hover/tap these!

Also back in 2016, I had my first solo book published - "Perverted Innocence".
It is a collection of stories mostly in line with the same Sci-Fi theme that characterizes my works. From apocalyptic world ending plots to psychological drama, I like to take the characters to the limit, with some crazy drawings to portray their far-fetched journeys.
The book also features my 4-page comic "Rock Paper Scissors: The Duel", which won a contest at Iberanime Lx2015.

Two years later, I was invited to enter a new collection - "Humanus" - with more amazing artists, some of which had also participated in the first collective book.
This time I did a small piece related to a theme of romance and drama.
During these years I had an incredible experience and for that I have to thank my editors Jorge and Sharon for believing in my potential and opening the doors to these great opportunities.
